Places to visit in Chicago

Downtown Chicago

Chicago, Illinois is my favorite place to visit in Illinois. It has a beautiful skyline and lots to sightsee. There are tons of museums and places to shop. The public transport can be taken to China Town and a bunch of other surrounding towns for an affordable price. Walking through downtown is a great way to spend your day.

Some of my favorite places to visit include:

  • The Lake Front
  • The Magnificent Mile
  • The Chicago River

Six Flags Great America

Six Flags is a great amusement park located in Gurnee Illinois. It is one of twenty seven locations in the United States with over 50 roller coasters. Not only does it have fun roller coasters, but there are fun carnival games and shows. Going to Six Flags is a full day activity that is fun to visit with friends.

It is one of the most famous beaches in the world because of the beautiful white sand and turquoise water.

Some of my favorite rides include:

  • Raging Bull
  • Goliath
  • SuperMan
  • X-Flight

Naperville

Naperville is one of Illinois' most popular suburbs. The riverwalk is the most visited location and runs through downtown naperville. Along the riverwalk, shopping boutiques, restaurants, and cute cafes can be found.

With the main trail taking you all the way up to the observation tower to overlook four types of the forest.

Some places to see in Naperville are:

  • Naperville River Walk
  • Naperville Public Library
  • Dave's Hot Chicken
